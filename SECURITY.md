## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please report suspected vulnerabilities **privately** by emailing
**security@yourdomain.com**.

To help us triage quickly, include:
- A clear description of the issue and potential impact
- Steps to reproduce (or a proof of concept)
- Affected versions/commit hashes and environment details
- Any suggested mitigation or fix (if available)

### What to expect
- **Acknowledgement:** within 3 business days
- **Initial triage/update:** within 7 business days
- **Status updates:** at least every 14 days until resolution

If the report is accepted, we will work on a fix and coordinate a disclosure
timeline with you. Please do not open public issues for unpatched
vulnerabilities.

If the report is out of scope or cannot be reproduced, we will let you know
with the reason whenever possible.
